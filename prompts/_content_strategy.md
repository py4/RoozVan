# RoozVan / روز وَن — Instagram Content Format Strategy

## 1. Page Goal

RoozVan / روز وَن is a Farsi-first Instagram page for Iranian diaspora and Persian-speaking immigrants living in Metro Vancouver, BC.

The page should feel like:

> A smart local Persian friend in Vancouver who follows the news and tells you what actually matters, in simple Farsi.

The goal is not to translate English news word-for-word.

The goal is to turn local information into:

- Useful Farsi explanations
- Local Vancouver/BC context
- Clear action steps
- Trustworthy immigrant-relevant updates
- Simple summaries for people who may not follow English news outlets

---

## 2. Core Editorial Promise

Every piece of content should answer at least one of these questions:

1. What happened?
2. Why does it matter to Iranians / Persian speakers in Vancouver?
3. Who is affected?
4. What should people do next?
5. When and where does this apply?
6. What is the trusted source?

If the content does not answer any of these, it probably should not be posted.

---

## 3. North Star

RoozVan should not feel like:

> Persian CBC

It should feel like:

> My smart Persian friend in Vancouver who reads the news for me and explains what matters.

The value is not copying the article.

The value is:

```text
English source → understand → simplify → localize → explain in Farsi → add action/context
```

# 4. Main Format Decision Rule

## Simple version

```text
Urgent + simple → Story

Useful + simple + permanent → Single post

Useful + complex + saveable → Carousel

Interesting but not very useful → Story or skip

Not relevant to Iranian diaspora in Metro Vancouver → Skip
```

## Mental model

| Format | Main job | Audience mindset | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| Story | Know this now | Quick glance | Alerts, weather, reminders, disruptions, same-day events |
| Single post | This happened | Awareness | Simple local news, announcements, community updates |
| Carousel | Understand this | Save/share/learn | Laws, immigration, housing, taxes, benefits, guides, explainers |
| Highlight | Find this later | Reference | Recurring resources, deadlines, guides, city services |
| Reel | Discover us | Discovery/connection | Short explainers, myth-busting, human/local tips |

---

# 5. Article Scoring Framework

For every article, score each signal from 0 to 2.

| Signal | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Local relevance | Not Metro Vancouver/BC | BC-wide but broad | Directly Metro Vancouver / Vancouver / Surrey / Burnaby / North Van |
| Diaspora usefulness | Generic news | Some relevance | Directly useful to immigrants / newcomers / Persian speakers |
| Urgency | No deadline | Relevant this week | Relevant today/tomorrow/next 72h |
| Actionability | No action | People may care | People need to do/check/avoid something |
| Complexity | Very simple | Some context needed | Needs explanation/timeline/numbers |
| Save/share potential | Low | Medium | High |
| Community value | Low | Medium | High |

## Format mapping

```text
If urgency >= 2 and complexity <= 1:
    format = story

Else if save_share_potential >= 2 or complexity >= 2:
    format = carousel

Else if local_relevance >= 1 and diaspora_usefulness >= 1:
    format = single_post

Else:
    format = skip_or_story
```

# 6. Format 1 — Story

## Use Story when

Use a Story for content that is urgent, temporary, simple, or useful in the moment.

Good examples:

- Weather warning
- Air quality / wildfire smoke alert
- Water restriction reminder
- Transit disruption
- Road closure
- Same-day public event
- Free community event
- Deadline reminder
- “Today in Vancouver” quick update

## Do not use Story-only when

Avoid Story-only if the content is:

- Legally important
- Financially important
- Immigration-related
- Healthcare-related
- Complex
- Something people may need to save
- Something people may want to send to family later

In those cases, use a carousel or single post, then share that post to Story.

---

## Story content rule

A Story image should contain:

1. Category label
2. One main takeaway
3. Why it matters
4. Action/date/location
5. Source/date

## Story word limit

```text
Ideal: 25–45 Farsi words
Maximum: 60 Farsi words
```

## Story image template

```text
[Category]
خبر مهم برای ونکوور

[Main takeaway]
از ۸ جون محدودیت مصرف آب در مترو ونکوور شدیدتر می‌شود.

[Why it matters]
آبیاری چمن و شست‌وشوی فضای بیرونی محدودتر خواهد شد.

[Action]
قبل از استفاده از آب در فضای بیرونی، قوانین شهر خودتان را چک کنید.

[Source]
منبع: CBC / Metro Vancouver — Jun 2
```

## Story design rule

Stories should be:

- 9:16
- High contrast
- Large readable Farsi text
- One visual idea
- No long paragraphs
- No full article
- No tiny footnotes
- No screenshot of English article
- No clickbait

## Story CTA options

```text
برای کسی که در ونکوور زندگی می‌کند بفرستید.
```

```text
این را برای بعد save کنید — پست کامل در صفحه.
```

```text
قوانین شهر خودتان را قبل از اقدام چک کنید.
```

# 7. Format 2 — Single Image Post

## Use single post when

Use a single post when the news is simple but worth keeping on the grid.

Good examples:

- Local leadership change
- Community announcement
- New public project
- City update
- Sports/culture news
- Local safety update
- Simple government announcement
- “This happened in Vancouver/BC”

## Single post content rule

The image should not explain everything.

The image should:

- Attract attention
- Summarize the core news
- Give one line of context
- Include source/date

The caption should explain:

1. What happened
2. Why it matters
3. Who is affected
4. Source
5. Any caveat or uncertainty

## Single post image template

```text
[Headline]
رئیس پلیس ساری کنار رفت؛ رئیس هیئت پلیس هم استعفا داد

[Context]
این اتفاق وسط بحران اخاذی‌ها و تغییر ساختار پلیس ساری رخ داده است.

[Source/date]
CBC | Jun 2026
```

## Caption template

```markdown
چی شده؟

[Short explanation in simple Farsi.]

چرا مهمه؟

[Why this matters to people living in Metro Vancouver / Surrey / BC.]

چه کسانی بیشتر تحت تأثیرند؟

[Residents, business owners, families, renters, drivers, students, etc.]

نکته مهم:

[Uncertainty / caveat / what is not known yet.]

منبع:
[Source name + date]
```

## When to upgrade a single post to carousel

Turn a single post into a carousel if:

- There are important numbers
- There is a timeline
- Multiple groups are affected
- People need to understand a process
- People may save/share it
- The topic involves law, immigration, rent, taxes, healthcare, schools, safety, benefits, or public services

# 8. Format 3 — Carousel Post

## Use carousel when

Use carousel for useful, complex, saveable, or explainable topics.

Best topics:

- Immigration updates
- BC law changes
- Rent and housing rules
- Tax and benefit deadlines
- Healthcare / MSP / pharmacy updates
- School / daycare updates
- ICBC / driving / licence changes
- Weather and emergency preparedness
- Public safety explainers
- City service guides
- “What this means for you” topics

## Carousel length

```text
Ideal: 5–7 slides
Maximum for normal news: 8 slides
Maximum for deep guides: 10 slides
```

Instagram may allow more carousel items, but RoozVan should usually keep carousels short enough for non-news-savvy readers to finish.

## Carousel structure

| Slide | Job |
|---|---|
| 1 | Hook / headline |
| 2 | What happened |
| 3 | Why it matters |
| 4 | Who is affected |
| 5 | Key numbers/dates |
| 6 | What to do |
| 7 | Source + caveat |

## Carousel template

```markdown
Slide 1:
[Hook]
قیمت بلیت‌های جام جهانی در ونکوور چقدر بالاست؟

Slide 2:
[What happened]
طبق گزارش CBC، قیمت بعضی بلیت‌ها در بازار فروش مجدد به صدها یا هزاران دلار رسیده.

Slide 3:
[Why it matters]
برای خیلی از ساکنان محلی، دیدن بازی از نزدیک ممکن است از نظر مالی سخت باشد.

Slide 4:
[Who is affected]
هواداران فوتبال، خانواده‌ها، تازه‌واردها و کسانی که دنبال تجربه جام جهانی در ونکوور هستند.

Slide 5:
[Useful detail]
بازی‌های پرتقاضا و بازی‌های حساس معمولاً گران‌تر هستند.

Slide 6:
[Action]
قبل از خرید، قیمت نهایی، قوانین resale، refund و منبع فروش را چک کنید.

Slide 7:
[Source]
منبع: CBC — Jun 2026
```

## Carousel CTA options

```text
این پست را برای کسی که دنبال بلیت جام جهانی است بفرستید.
```

```text
برای بعد save کنید.
```

```text
اگر در ساری زندگی می‌کنید، این را برای همسایه/دوستتان بفرستید.
```

# 9. Format 4 — Highlights

## Use Highlights for

Highlights are your permanent library.

Recommended Highlights:

```text
مهاجرت
قوانین BC
اجاره‌خانه
آب‌وهوا
حمل‌ونقل
سلامت
مالیات
رویدادها
ونکوور 101
ساری / برنابی / نورث ون
```

## What goes into Highlights

Add stories to Highlights only if they are useful after 24 hours.

Good Highlight content:

- Guide summaries
- Recurring rules
- City services
- Useful links
- Evergreen explainers
- Deadline reminders until the deadline passes

Do not add every breaking news story to Highlights.

# 10. Format 5 — Reel

## Use Reel when

Use Reels for discovery and human connection, not dense information.

Good Reel ideas:

- “۳ چیزی که تازه‌واردها در ونکوور باید بدانند”
- “این قانون جدید BC یعنی چی؟”
- “اگر انگلیسی‌ات قوی نیست، این خبر برای تو مهمه”
- “اشتباه رایج درباره اجاره‌خانه در ونکوور”
- “خلاصه خبر امروز در ۳۰ ثانیه”

## Reel rule

If the topic requires exact dates, legal details, or step-by-step instructions, make the Reel a teaser and point to a carousel.

# 12. News Article Processing Pipeline

## Step 1 — Extract facts

For each article, extract:

```json
{
  "title": "",
  "source": "",
  "date": "",
  "location": "",
  "main_event": "",
  "affected_people": [],
  "key_numbers": [],
  "key_dates": [],
  "action_items": [],
  "uncertainties": [],
  "source_url": ""
}
```

## Step 2 — Score relevance

```json
{
  "local_relevance": 0,
  "diaspora_usefulness": 0,
  "urgency": 0,
  "actionability": 0,
  "complexity": 0,
  "save_share_potential": 0,
  "community_value": 0
}
```

## Step 3 — Select format

```python
if urgency >= 2 and complexity <= 1:
    format = "story"
elif save_share_potential >= 2 or complexity >= 2:
    format = "carousel"
elif local_relevance >= 1 and diaspora_usefulness >= 1:
    format = "single_post"
else:
    format = "skip_or_story"
```

## Step 4 — Generate content

Each format should generate different content.

```json
{
  "story": {
    "headline": "",
    "why_it_matters": "",
    "action": "",
    "source_line": ""
  },
  "single_post": {
    "image_headline": "",
    "image_context": "",
    "caption": ""
  },
  "carousel": {
    "slides": [
      "hook",
      "what_happened",
      "why_it_matters",
      "who_is_affected",
      "key_numbers_dates",
      "what_to_do",
      "source_caveat"
    ],
    "caption": ""
  }
}
```

# 13. Editorial Rules

## Always do

- Write in simple, natural Farsi
- Add Vancouver/BC context
- Explain why it matters
- Mention source and date
- Add uncertainty when facts are not confirmed
- Prefer government/official sources when possible
- Use CBC / CityNews / government / official sources as inputs, but transform the content into RoozVan’s own explanation
- For important topics, add a caption with more context
- For feed posts, add alt text
- For complex topics, choose carousel instead of cramming everything into one image

## Never do

- Do not paste the full article into an image
- Do not screenshot English articles as the main content
- Do not copy long paragraphs from the source
- Do not use copyrighted photos unless you have permission or a clear license
- Do not exaggerate or create panic
- Do not translate ambiguous claims as facts
- Do not make political content rage-bait
- Do not post every local article just because it is local
- Do not make the image text so dense that people cannot read it on mobile

# 14. Writing Style

## Voice

RoozVan’s voice should be:

```text
clear
calm
useful
local
trustworthy
not dramatic
not childish
not overly formal
```

## Farsi style

Use conversational but respectful Persian.

Prefer:

```text
چی شده؟
چرا مهمه؟
چه کسانی تحت تأثیرند؟
حالا باید چی کار کنیم؟
```

Avoid:

```text
بحران بی‌سابقه!
فاجعه در ونکوور!
همه شوکه شدند!
حتماً ببینید!
```

## Translation rule

Do not translate word-for-word from English.

Instead:

```text
English article → understand → simplify → localize → explain in Farsi
```

# 18. Publishing Rhythm

## Daily baseline

```text
Stories: 2–5 per day, spread across the day
Feed posts: 1 per day or 4–6 per week
Carousels: 2–4 per week
Reels: 1–3 per week if capacity allows
```

## Do not post everything at once

Spread stories throughout the day:

```text
Morning: weather / transit / urgent reminder
Midday: useful news
Evening: summary / community / event / repost feed post
```

## Story your posts

When you publish a feed post or carousel, also share it to Story with a short reason.

Examples:

```text
اگر در ونکوور زندگی می‌کنید، این پست مهمه 👇
```

```text
خلاصه ساده این تغییر جدید در BC را اینجا گذاشتیم 👇
```

# 21. Copyright and Source Safety

## Do

- Create original Farsi summaries
- Use your own design
- Use licensed images, original illustrations, or generated visuals when appropriate
- Cite source name and date
- Link/source in caption when possible
- Use short paraphrased summaries instead of long copied text

## Avoid

- Copying the full article
- Translating the full article line-by-line
- Using publisher images without permission
- Screenshotting English news pages
- Making it look like RoozVan is the original reporting source when it is not

## Safe source line examples

```text
منبع: CBC News — Jun 2, 2026
```

```text
منبع: Government of British Columbia — Jun 2026
```

```text
بر اساس گزارش CBC و اطلاعات منتشرشده توسط Metro Vancouver
```
